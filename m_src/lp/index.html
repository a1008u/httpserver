XLARGE x Dragon Ball Z Tees

By Dragon Ball Z  XLARGE 

Two years since their last collaboration with the popular anime franchise, the always-active Tokyo branch of the LA-based streetwear pioneer XLARGE is set to release another series of Japan-exclusive tees emblazoned with Dragon Ball Z characters and logos.

Available in three designs and three different colors each, these XLARGE x Dragon Ball Z Tees feature the fan favorites Vegeta, Trunks, and Majin Buu. The former two designs play on XLARGE’s “OG Gorilla” logo with lettering reminiscent of Dragon Ball Z’s. The latter design borrows Majin Buu’s salmon pink skin tone for color options and fonts.
